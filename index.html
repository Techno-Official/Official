Our Development Process

  • 01 Initial Planning

    We won’t just build you a website, we look in to the fine details that will help your website serve a purpose, whether that be generating sales, finding leads, spreading your message or any other need! We also discuss similar websites in your industry that you find appealing.

  • 02 Content Creation

    There are some things we need your help with and content creation is the main one! You can send us the text you want on your website in a Google or Word document. Once we receive this, we can begin designing the layout of your pages so that they flow naturally and achieve your business goals.

  • 03 Design & Develop

    Now we can start developing your website. We start from scratch, building your homepage and all of the other necessary pages you need for your website. We will keep you updated every step of the way, so you can make changes as and when you please.

  • 04 Training & Launch

    Once your site is ready, we provide you with full training on how to maintain it with ease. You can book a complete training session with us in which we answer any questions you may have. Prior to launching your website, we test everything extensively to ensure you aren’t hit with any unexpected surprises. Once the final final payment is made, your site will be up, running and ready to go!
